These are some of the paintings that I have done. My paintings are passion projects, each reflecting my love for nature and the subtle beauty found in everyday life. I have two main mediums for my paintings; acrylic and watercolor, using each medium to explore different aspects of color, form, and emotion. With acrylics, I make bolder compositions and explore using strong vibrant colors to create a contrast. With my watercolors pieces I focus on the delicacy and softness of nature, capturing the quieter hues of life. Across all of my paintings I aim to highlight the beauty of the world we have using a variety of styles. I am continually experimenting with new concepts and techniques to bring each vision to life.
