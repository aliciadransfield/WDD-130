Drawing

These are some of my drawings and illustrations, ranging from quick sketches and studies to fully realized pieces. My sketches, often done in pencil or pen, focus more on human features, expression, and elements of nature, allowing me to capture the raw details in an exploitative way. When it comes to full on illustrations, I primarily use digital tools, charcoal, or colored media to create expressive snapshots in time. My work often explores the connections of life, highlighting emotions and occasionally stories. Whether done through linework or atmospheric compositions I always aim for a sense of presence in each piece.
